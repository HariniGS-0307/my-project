Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.
How do I schedule an appointment?
Navigate to the Appointments section and click "Schedule New". Select the patient, doctor, date, and time for the appointment.
How do I generate a report?
Go to the Reports section and select the type of report you need. Choose the date range and click "Generate Report".
How does the medication reminder system work?
Our AI-powered system sends automated reminders via SMS, email, and in-app notifications 10 minutes before medication time and continues every 5 minutes until the patient confirms they've taken their medication.
How do I add a new patient to the system?
Go to the Patients section and click "Add New Patient". Fill in the required details including medical history and current prescriptions. The AI system will automatically generate a medication schedule based on the information provided.
